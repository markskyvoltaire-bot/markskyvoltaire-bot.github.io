About

Sky is a Washington DC-based emcee with Haitian and French roots, blending lived experience, ancestry, and lyrical precision into something unique. He started with NorthStar International and has worked with artists from Sleepwalker (Shamans of Sound, Krononauts, etc), Haezl, and Jahson the Scientist. A former chef, genealogist, and lifelong student of culture, he brings depth and intentionality into every verse, every flow, every sound.
