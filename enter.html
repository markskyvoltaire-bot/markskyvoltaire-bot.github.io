About Me

Born in Paris, raised between Haiti, Chicago, and Washington D.C., my life has been a mix of cultures, experiences, and stories that shape my music today.

Music and storytelling became my way of connecting with the world. From Northstar International to solo work, every track and lyric is a reflection of my journey.
